Attachment tier shifts
The addition of Blue/Violet lasers or Light barrels attachments will each decrease your tier by -1 (improves accuracy) Having "ADS Accuracy While Moving" penalty attachments increases your tier by +1 (decreases accuracy). By default, only the +0 case is shown.

Burst compare only affects the spread compare plot. In burst mode, the main shots slider becomes the number of bursts, and the graph still shows pre-shot spread only. The burst wait slider adds extra delay beyond normal shot cadence, so 0.00 s matches the fastest possible re-start.
Standing is plotted once per weapon as a solid line. Moving cases use dashed lines. Weapon A and Weapon B use different marker symbols and color families so both weapon sets can coexist on the same graph clearly. The effective spread increase overlay shows the shot-to-shot net increase in pre-shot spread, which helps reveal how much a weapon is actually blooming after decay between rounds.
